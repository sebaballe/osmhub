📊 Statistical Verdict
Arsenal dominates Barcelona in every metric. It wouldn't even be close.
🏆 Title Race
Arsenal is running away with this tournament. They lead in shots (13/game) AND possession (61.5%). No team has both. That combination wins leagues.
Fenerbahce controls possession better than any team except Arsenal. 61% is elite. If they convert more chances, they're the biggest threat.
Boca Juniors doesn't need the ball. 12 shots on 49.5% possession means they're brutally efficient. The kind of team that beats you and you never see it coming.
⚠️ Teams at Risk
Barcelona is in crisis. 5 shots. 37.5% possession. Worst numbers in the tournament — and it's not close. Major tactical rethink needed.
River Plate and Galatasaray are barely competing. Both under 7 shots and under 45% possession. Getting outplayed in almost every game.
Santos and Juventus are too passive. 7 shots/game won't cut it in a 16-team league. They need to create more.
🔍 Tactical Patterns
The possession trap: AC Milan has 60.5% possession but results don't match. High possession doesn't always mean dominance.
Counter-attack kings: Boca Juniors and Benfica generate high shots despite below-average possession. Speed over control — and it's working.
The quiet middle: Ajax and Bayern sit in the middle of every metric. Competitive, not dominant. They'll decide the middle of the table.
